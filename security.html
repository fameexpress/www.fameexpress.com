Our Security Commitments

These are the specific, concrete practices we follow when handling your Amazon Ads data — no vague promises.

🔑

Read-Only Access — Always

We only request read-only access to your advertising data. We cannot make changes to your campaigns, budgets, bids, or any other settings. Our access is scoped to read your performance data and AMC signals — nothing more. You remain in full control of your account at all times.

🏢

Your Data Is Never Shared

Your data is processed securely and never shared with third parties. We do not share, sell, license, or otherwise transfer your advertising data to any external parties, partners, or platforms — under any circumstances. Your competitive data stays yours.

🚫

We Never Sell Your Data

We never sell, share, or use your data for any purpose other than generating your analysis. Your data is not used to train models, benchmark against other clients, or generate insights for anyone other than you. Full stop.

🔐

Encrypted in Transit

All access is authenticated and encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2+. Data flowing between Amazon's APIs, our analysis infrastructure, and our reporting systems is protected with industry-standard transport layer security. We do not transmit your data over unencrypted channels.

🗑️

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained only for the duration of our engagement. Once our work together concludes, your data is deleted from our systems. If you need your data deleted sooner, just ask — we will honor deletion requests promptly, no questions asked.

Working Within Amazon's Official Frameworks

We don't use workarounds or unofficial scraping. Our data access is fully sanctioned and operates within Amazon's published API guidelines.

🟠 Amazon API & AMC Partner

Fame Express operates exclusively within Amazon's official Advertising API and Amazon Marketing Cloud (AMC) frameworks. This means our access to your data is governed by Amazon's own security, authentication, and compliance requirements — providing an additional layer of oversight beyond our own practices.

AMC is a privacy-safe, cloud-based clean room solution provided and operated by Amazon. All queries run inside Amazon's infrastructure. We receive aggregated outputs — never raw user-level data — in strict compliance with Amazon's data use policies.

When you grant us access, you're using the same OAuth mechanisms Amazon provides to all verified advertising partners. You can revoke our access at any time directly from your Amazon Advertising account settings.

Our Compliance Roadmap

We're a focused expert team, not a sprawling enterprise. Here's where we are and where we're headed.

🏛️ Working Toward SOC 2 Type II

We are actively working toward SOC 2 Type II certification, which will provide independent, third-party validation of our security controls, availability, and data handling practices. This is a deliberate investment in enterprise trust infrastructure.

In the meantime, we follow the principles of SOC 2 Trust Services Criteria — including access control, monitoring, incident response, and change management — as operating standards within our team.
